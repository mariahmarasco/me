"Parenthood" Short Film

Vimeo Password: NYC2020

Logline: Separated by distance, Chelsea and Brandon discover the challenges of parenthood.

Description: "Parenthood" tells the story of two young parents separated unexpectedly by distance and the challenges they face while trying to give their child everything. Created during the quarantine, this film was shot remotely and directed over Zoom.
