DIRECTOR | MOTION DESIGNER

Mariah is a Director and Motion Designer from Boston living in New York City. Her technical and creative background includes a B.A. in Graphic Design and 3 years of Art Direction in advertising. After falling in love with time-based narratives, her goal is to persue a career in the entertainment industry using film and animation to create empathy and highlight hidden stories.

As a storyteller at heart, she is constantly on the lookout for ways to use the 2-dimensional format as a way of creating multi-dimensional worlds built around characters who exhibit unique human (or in-human) truths.

Her directing and video editing work has led her to complete projects within documentary/interview as well as short narratives for BBC Studios, The New School's Welcome Center, and Wilde TV.
